\section{3. Pose Tracking Based on Optial
Flow}\label{pose-tracking-based-on-optial-flow}

For Multi-Person Pose tracking in videos:\\
1. estimates human poses in frames 2. track these human pose by
assigneing a unique identification number

** greedy matching algorithm **:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  assign the id of \(P_i^{-1}\) in fram \(I^{k-1}\) to \(P_j^k\) in
  frame \(I^k\) if the similarity between \(P_i^{-1}\) and \(P_j^k\) is
  the highest\\
\item
  remove these two instances and repeat id assigning porcess with the
  highest similarity.
\end{itemize}

** there are two difference between out implementation and above.**\\
1. two kinds of human boxes, one is from a human detector, the other is
generated from previous frames. 2. use a flow-based pose similarity
metric.

\subsection{3.1 Joint Propagation using Optical
Flow}\label{joint-propagation-using-optical-flow}

\begin{quote}
光流：
通过检测图像像素点的强度随时间变化进而推断出物体移动速度及方向的方法
\end{quote}

Due to Motion blur or occlusoin, the single detector in optical flow may
lead to missing detections and false detections. so \textbf{Add the
processng frame from nearby frames using temporal information expressed
in optical flow}.\\
\#\# 3.2 Flow\_based Pose Similarity\\
之前是使用IoU(Intersection-over-Union)作为相似性度量，这在实例运动很快没有重叠或者人群中的实例没什么关系时会有问题。对此有一个更精准的：\\
\textbf{Pose similarity}: calculates the body joints distacne between
two intstances using Object Keypoint Similarity(OKS)\\
但是Pose
similarity在不同帧上人的姿态改变时也会有问题，为此我们使用flow-based
pose similarity.\\
  \$ S\_\{Flow\}(J\_i\^{}k, J\_j\^{}l) = OKS(\{\hat J\}\_i\^{}l,
J\_i\^{}l)\$   (1)\\
其中的关键在于\({\hat J}_i^l\), 代表着上一节中的propagated joints for
\(J_i^k\) from frame \(I^k\) to \(I^l\) using optical flow feild
\(F_{k\to l}\).\\
在视频中，有时人还可能消失和重新出现，为此两个frame（帧）之间的flow不够用，我们可以利用之前的多个帧。\\
\#\# 3.3 Flow-based Pose Tracking Algorithm 算法流程： 1.
解决姿态估计问题，在处理每一帧时，将之前用propagating joints
产生的box与human detector产生的box用Non-Maximum
Suppression合起来，基于此做姿态估计。 2.
解决姿态追踪的问题，在第k帧时，我们计算未被追踪的身体关节实例\(J^k\)和之前帧存储的实例Q之间的flow-based
pose similarity matrix \(M_{sim}\), 然后对每个关节分配id，
得到实例\(P^k\)，将其加入Q中。
